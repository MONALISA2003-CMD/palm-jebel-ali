About the Address

Eight Fronds, One Quiet Centre

Palm Jebel Ali unfolds across sixteen fronds reaching into the Arabian Gulf, each one a private arm of sand and water. Frond N sits toward the heart of this arrangement, close enough to the trunk to feel connected, far enough along the curve to feel apart from it. Palm Central Private Residences occupies this position with a low collection of homes that face the sea on one side and a landscaped promenade on the other.

The Developer

Nakheel

Nakheel shaped the Dubai coastline the world now recognises, building Palm Jumeirah from open water into one of the most visited man made islands on earth. Palm Jebel Ali extends that work at twice the scale, applying three decades of waterfront engineering and placemaking to a new stretch of coast.

Palm Jumeirah values have risen by more than eighty percent across five years. Palm Central Private Residences carries that same lineage, the same engineering discipline, and the same long view on what waterfront living in Dubai is worth.

Lifestyle

A Day Measured in Light

Mornings begin on a terrace facing east, breakfast carried out as the lagoon turns from grey to blue. The private beach stays uncrowded by design, a short walk from every residence rather than a destination to plan toward. Evenings settle into the promenade, where palms line a stretch of pathway built for nothing more than a slow walk before dinner.

Inside, ceilings lift higher than is typical for the category, glass runs floor to ceiling along every sea facing wall, and finishes lean toward warm stone and pale timber rather than anything that competes with the view.
